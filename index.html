About me

Hello! I’m Rahul Varma, a dedicated and passionate second-year Computer Science Engineering (CSE) student at Vignan’s Institute of Information Technology, Visakhapatnam. My academic journey began at Chaitanya Public School in the Steel Plant area of Visakhapatnam, where I completed my 10th class with an impressive GPA of 9.5.

Fuelled by a fascination for mechanics and how things work, I pursued a Diploma in Mechanical Engineering at Sri Chaitanya Engineering College in Kommadi, Visakhapatnam, securing a commendable 80%. This experience solidified my foundation in engineering principles.

Currently, I am honing my technical acumen in CSE, where I am not only learning about the theoretical aspects of computing but also actively engaging in practical applications. My goal is to become a proficient software engineer with a specialization in full-stack development. I am eager to apply my knowledge in creating innovative solutions that can make a difference in the tech world.
